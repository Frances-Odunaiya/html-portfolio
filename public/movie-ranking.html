My Best Movies in 2023 according to Frances

Below are my 3 most favorites movies this year 2023

Blood Ties

The spanish series about three sisters trained to avenge their mothers death. I love the sisterly bond they share.

Blood & Water

This is about a family willing to do everything to find their eldest child who was a victim of child trafficking. I love South African movies.

BRIDGERTON - Queen Charlotte

This is one of the Bridgerton stories about the mad king. It emphasized the love that he shared with his wife during his reign and also how supportive the queen was.
